[RKNN-Toolkit2](https://github.com/rockchip-linux/rknn-toolkit2.git) is a software development kit for users to perform model conversion, 
inference and performance evaluation on PC and Rockchip NPU platforms 
* RK3566/RK3568, 
* RK3588/RK3588S, 
* RV1103/RV1106, 
* RK3562, etc.
