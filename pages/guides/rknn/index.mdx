**RKNN-Toolkit2** is a software development kit for users to perform model conversion, 
inference and performance evaluation on PC and Rockchip NPU platforms

Primarily supporting the following platforms:

* RK3566/RK3568, 
* RK3588/RK3588S, 
* RV1103/RV1106, 
* RK3562, etc.

Find out more at: [rockchip-linux/rknn-toolkit2](https://github.com/rockchip-linux/rknn-toolkit2.git)
